Here are some precautions that you can take during floods:

1. If you live in a flood-prone area or are camping in a low-lying area, move to higher ground immediately.
2. If you are told to evacuate, do so immediately. If you must evacuate your home, take only essential items and bring your pets if safe to do so. Do not walk through moving water.
3. If there is a possibility of a flash flood, move to higher ground. Lock your home when you leave. If you have time, disconnect utilities and appliances. Turn off water, gas, and electrical services before leaving.
